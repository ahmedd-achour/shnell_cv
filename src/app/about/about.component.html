Our Mission

Revolutionizing Road Safety in Tunisia

Founded in 2018, Car Safety was born from a simple yet powerful idea: that every driver in Tunisia deserves immediate help when they need it most. Our founder witnessed firsthand the critical difference that rapid emergency response makes in saving lives on the road.

Today, we've grown into the country's leading automotive safety service, combining cutting-edge technology with compassionate human support to protect drivers across Tunisia.
