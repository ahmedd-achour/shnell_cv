Frequently Asked Questions

Our emergency assistance is available 24/7. In case of an accident, press the emergency button in the app or call our hotline. We'll immediately dispatch help and notify emergency contacts with your location.

You can register your vehicle through our app or website. You'll need your vehicle registration details, insurance information, and emergency contact information. Our team will guide you through the process.

When an emergency is triggered, we share your location, vehicle details, medical information (like blood type and allergies), and emergency contact information with relevant emergency services to ensure you receive proper care.
